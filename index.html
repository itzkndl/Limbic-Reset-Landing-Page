How it works

Four steps. One daily reset.

1

Take the quiz

7 quick questions about how you're feeling right now. We build your personalized card feed from your answers — no two feeds are the same.

2

Swipe your cards

Swipe right to save what lands. Left to skip. Heart your favorites. The choice is active — that's what makes it stick.

3

Get cards delivered

Resetters choose their delivery frequency — daily, weekly, or monthly. Your saved and favorite cards show up in your inbox or by text. Wanderers can explore but cards won't be delivered.

4

Build your streak

Streaks, badges, and a growing vault of saved cards. 7-day, 30-day, 100-day — keep going.
